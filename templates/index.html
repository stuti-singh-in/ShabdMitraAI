ShabdMitra

Your personalized language tutor powered by AI


Take your language skills to new heights with ShabdMitra!


ShabdMitra is an innovative Indian AI language learning website that provides personalized learning experiences to users. The website uses AI technology to have a conversation with the user in their target language at a customized difficulty level. After the conversation, the AI analyzes the user's mistakes and creates a personalized vocabulary quiz to help them learn from their errors. This unique approach to language learning offers a fun and engaging way to improve language proficiency. ]With ShabdMitra, users can easily practice speaking, listening, and vocabulary in their target language while receiving immediate feedback from the AI.
